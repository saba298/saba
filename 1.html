General Questions

I am not a resident of the United Arab Emirates. Can I open an account with the Bank?

Non-residents can open Savings Account with the bank.
As per guidelines issued by The Central Bank of UAE, GCC nationals are allowed to open Current Account.

How do I subscribe for E-Statements?

Subscription of E-Statements is done through HAWK Online. Simply Login and click on “More” then “E-Statements” in the “Other Services” section

Can I do all of my banking with you?

Yes. Thanks to the convenience of Debit Cards, Online Banking, Bill Payments, Automatic Transfers, everyday banking activities can be performed on your own time and convenience.
